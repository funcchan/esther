About

I'm Ryan Kaskel. Welcome to my humble home on the web!

Here are a few facts about me:

  • Born January 22, 1987 in Miami, Florida, USA (I'm twenty-four years old)
  • Attended Miami-Palmetto Senior High School
  • Graduated in 2010 from Tufts University in Boston, Massachusetts with a Bachelor of Arts
  • I majored in History
  • I have an M.A. in Medieval History from King’s College London

  • emacs 24 with Workrave RSI prevention software.
  • org-mode for emacs; a lifesaver for daily organization

  • virtualenv/ virtualenvwrapper - Python interpreter isolation for the sane developer.
  • LastPass
  • Quicklisp, SLIME, and SBCL - for all my Common Lisp needs. I am learning the language using the excellent book Practical Common Lisp by Peter Seibel.
  • Git

You can find me on Google, GitHub, and Launchpad. I also have a Twitter account, but rarely use it.
